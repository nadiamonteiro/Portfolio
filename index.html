About Me


I am a recent graduate from the The University of Kent. I studied Law and Criminology, followed by a Master's in Computer Science. My undergraduate degree left me with a set of transferable skills such as analytical thinking, problem solving skills, attention to detail, strong written and verbal skills. I have recently been spending my time engaging in web development.

Over the course of my 4 years pursuing university degrees, I have always excelled at delivering before deadlines. I was really active in societies, and even attended workshops to hone my communication and interpersonal skills. Balancing my extra curricular activities with my university work helped me develop skills around prioritization and time management which are essential to this role.

Outside of working with technology, I can be found working out in the gym, trying my hand at new recipes in the kitchen or winding down by watching T.V. shows and movies. I also really enjoy reading books, and the power they hold to transport me into a new world with just a few words. I have also recently taken an interest in illustration and graphic design, which I find to be a great creative outlet.
